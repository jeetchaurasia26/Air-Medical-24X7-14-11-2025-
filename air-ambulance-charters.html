What Does an Air Ambulance Charter Cost?

The cost of air ambulance services in India depends on several key factors:

  • Distance Covered: The longer the route, the higher the cost.
  • Aircraft Type: Jets, turboprops, or helicopters have varying costs depending on speed and medical equipment.
  • Medical Equipment Required: ICU-level flights with ventilators and life-support devices cost more than basic transfers.
  • Medical Team Composition: More complex cases requiring multiple doctors or specialists will affect pricing.
  • Ground Ambulance Transfers: Ground-to-airport and hospital transfers are included as part of end-to-end service.
