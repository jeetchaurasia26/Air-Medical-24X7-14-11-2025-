Medical Escort Services

From ground transfers to commercial flight escorts and dedicated air ambulance support, Air Medical 24x7 offers professional, bed-to-bed escort services for stable and non-critical patients worldwide.

What Is a Medical Escort Service?

A medical escort service provides a trained medical professional who travels with the patient (by ground vehicle or by air — commercial or dedicated) to monitor, treat, and support the patient throughout the journey.

Medical Escort in Air Ambulance

For non-critical patients who are fit to fly seated on a regular passenger flight, a medical escort can offer continuous monitoring and clinical care during travel. Our escorts are experienced doctors, nurses, or paramedics, equipped with essential medical tools and medications.

Why Have a Medical Escort?

Air transport allows quick transfer of patients from remote or medically underserved locations to specialized care centers. Having a skilled medical escort ensures safety, medical supervision, and peace of mind throughout the journey.

  • Relief & Personal Attention: 1:1 care and companionship throughout the trip.
  • Medical Oversight: Continuous monitoring and immediate response when needed.
  • Equipment & Support: Escorts carry oxygen, monitors, and essential emergency supplies.
