Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

1. Introduction

At Air Medical 24X7 Air Ambulance Services, we are fully committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, contact details, billing address, passport/ID, and payment information.
  • Medical Information: Patient diagnosis, treatment details, hospital reports, and insurance data.
  • Website & Technical Data: IP, browser, OS, cookies, and site usage analytics.
  • Communication Data: Records of emails, chats, calls, and feedback submissions.

3. How We Use the Information

We use your data to coordinate air ambulance operations, communicate updates, comply with legal requirements, enhance services, and—if consented—share marketing offers.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Data may be shared only with authorized partners such as hospitals, flight operators, and payment processors. We never sell or rent personal data to any third party.

5. Data Security

All data is secured via encryption, restricted employee access, and PCI-DSS compliant gateways. Despite strong measures, no system is entirely immune to breaches.

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to improve website functionality and analyze traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Services are intended for individuals aged 18+. If any minor data is collected, it will be deleted upon request.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill services, legal obligations, or dispute resolutions, after which it is deleted securely.

9. Your Rights

You have rights to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact info@airmedical24x7.com to exercise these rights.

10. Payment, Cancellation & Refund Policy

All services are 100% prepaid and strictly non-refundable. Payment confirmation is sent via email within 30 minutes of successful payment.

11. External Links

Our site may contain external links. Air Medical 24X7 is not responsible for third-party privacy policies or content.

12. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will reflect the new “Effective Date.”

13. Disclaimer

Air Medical 24X7 solely manages this Privacy Policy. Third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay) are not responsible for internal compliance.
