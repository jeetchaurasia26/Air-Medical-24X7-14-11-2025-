Air Ambulance Services in Andorra

24x7 ICU-Equipped Air Ambulance • International Repatriation • Medical Evacuations

Air Medical 24x7 provides 24/7 ICU-equipped air ambulance services from Andorra to over 100 countries worldwide. Since Andorra does not have its own airport, all operations are conducted via nearby airports in Spain and France — primarily La Seu d’Urgell Airport (LEU) in Spain and Toulouse-Blagnac Airport (TLS) in France.

We offer private air ambulance charters, commercial airline stretcher services, emergency medical flights, and international patient repatriation. Whether for residents, tourists, or business travelers, we ensure safe, fast, and fully monitored medical transfers from hospital to hospital.
